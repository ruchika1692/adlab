Sub Header:

We’re not a big agency.

A corporate recruitment environment is a brilliant place to establish your career. But it does get old, doesn’t it?

For us, it can’t compare with having freedom, accountability, and autonomy in a space where every success is visible and your ideas are actually heard.

Here, you’ll learn niche markets, establish close client partnerships, and develop in your career faster than you could have imagined. We’re fully remote and totally invested in what we do – we do a great job because we love it. 

You can come from any recruitment background, you’ll learn our markets soon enough. What’s important is that you genuinely want to be a better recruiter and have an insatiable drive to succeed.

You’ll also get:
  • Open holiday (25 in your contract)
  • £50 gym contribution (after probation)
  • Phone, MacBook, and whatever else you need to do your job
  • £500 annual training budget with Trevor Binder
  • Corporate credit card for entertaining clients and candidates (with sensible limit)
  • £200 towards dinner/drinks when you hit your targets (and a Taste card regardless)
  • Annual salary reviews
  • Up to 25% commission
If you’re an agency recruiter bored of being just a number, get in touch today.
