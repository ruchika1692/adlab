Case Studies

Martech, We exist to help you!

Sub Header:

Recruitment but better.

Ups and downs in hiring are inevitable, but our process is smoother by design. 

We know our market inside and out, so we don’t spend valuable time building networks from scratch. We understand where the best people can be found and the nuances of each company’s culture – so we can match the right people, to the right roles, at the right time.

Keenly aware of our D&I responsibilities, company and industry inequities, and which skills are underrepresented by candidates, we work with clients to close the gap. 

Experience matters for everyone, so we use technology to ramp up our efficiency without losing what makes us human. By having meaningful conversations and aligning candidate needs with companies who can meet them – each person becomes committed to your process. So when you’re ready to make an offer, they’re ready to say yes.

Our products.

Recruitment re-engineered.

Exclusive

‍The full works for your most critical and exec level hires. We agree on service deliverables, create fully branded candidate packs by our inhouse designers, run regular structured meetings to keep you informed, and much more. Our deepest search ever engages the superior talent to take your business to new heights, and our data-led approach offers insight on future performance. 

‍Classic

‍A targeted search service that enhances your employer brand and reputation by delivering an exceptional candidate experience. Our cutting-edge recruitment technology ensures a smooth process and offers insight into future candidate performance.

Startup

‍Designed for busy and bootstrapped startups – spread the cost of your hiring over 12 months, book interview slots in advance to accommodate your busy schedule, maximise efficiency with video technology, and much more. Your dedicated account manager will work as hard as you do to guarantee your results.

‍Contract

‍Our classic contract search services give you access to a flexible workforce of highly skilled contractors on a time and materials basis – to support complex tasks and help you meet project deliverables and deadlines. You’ll also get a free IR35 review for total peace of mind.

‍SOW

‍Outsource parts of your data and software projects at a fraction of the cost of traditional IT consultancies. Our fast, fully managed consultancy gives you access to our skilled network of project leaders who can deliver outcome-based packages of work, transferring knowledge to your existing teams as they go.
